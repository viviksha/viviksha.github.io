About Me

Viviksha is an undergraduate senior at Pratt Institute, Brooklyn, NY pursuing a BFA in Communication Design with a concentration in Advertising and Art direction. Born in India, raised in New Jersey, grew up in New York, Viviksha is an insightful designer constantly seeking for new ideologies to inspire her work. With an elevated interest in advertising, graphic design and illustration, she aims to design her clients’ vision by finding a distinct visual language in their needs.

Persona

Photo Series

Study of Human Personality

It has long been believed that each one of us is different. But what really makes each one us different? Conducting a survey, I found the immediate response was personality. Yes, personality is key to defining who we are as in definition it is the combination of characteristics and qualities that form an individual's distinctive character. But what makes our personality? Are we born with it? If we are, then how come we develop our persona with life’s challenges? Looking for these answers I explored a photo series of human persona; using one person and presenting them with a distinct personality trait. The fruits and vegetables communicate each personality type through their vivacious flavor, texture, and color.

In this fascinating adventure of human persona, I discovered how I can look and be perceived so differently when I present myself in diverse avatars. Personality conjectures behavior, which exposes a person’s innate thoughts and feelings. A human’s face is a lexicon of emotions that immediately conveys what emotions run through their minds. Therefore, along with an inner perception of ourselves, human identity is defined by the way we choose to move, the way we choose to dress, and the way we express ourselves which reflects our personality making each one us unique and singular yet relatable to each other.

Photography and Model: Viviksha Panchal

Original Design Studios

Photoshoot

Original Design Studio is a digital branding agency specializing in Graphic Design for E-Commerce and fashion-related brands, Photography and Social Media Marketing.

In redesigning the website of ODS, we maintained the main aesthetic of the studio: All forms of design intersect with one another and can be combined to achieve original concepts and unique design executions.

We utilized prominent design platforms – fine art, graphic design and photography – each presented thoroughly by distinct poses, garments, hair and makeup. The white wardrobe for the model and white background define purity of each platform. Her geometric dress, sturdy body language and sharp makeup lines characterize graphic design whereas long sleek dress with soft movements and organic hair and makeup personify photography. The orange smoke signifying fine art translate the mixture of each platform as it moves in the space combining foreground and background.

Art Direction: Viviksha Panchal and Gina Capozza

Photography: Robert Scheuerman

Model: Anna Ilina
