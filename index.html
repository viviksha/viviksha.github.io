About Me

Viviksha is an undergraduate senior at Pratt Institute, Brooklyn, NY pursuing a BFA in Communication Design with a concentration in Advertising Art Direction. Born in India, raised in New Jersey, grew up in New York, she is an insightful designer constantly seeking for new ideologies to inspire her work. With an elevated interest in advertising, graphic design and photography, she aims to design her clients’ vision by finding a distinct visual language in their needs.

Persona

Photo Series

Study of Human Personality

What really makes each one us different? Conducting a survey, the immediate response I received was personality. Personality in definition is a combination of characteristics and qualities that form an individual's character. But what makes our personality? What makes who we are? Looking for these answers I explored a photo series of human persona; using one person and presenting them with a distinct personality trait. The fruits and vegetables communicate each personality type through their distinct flavor, color and texture.

Photography and Model: Viviksha Panchal


In this fascinating adventure, I discovered how a human’s face is a lexicon of emotions that instantly conveys innate thoughts and feelings. One person can look and be perceived so differently when presented in diverse avatars. Identically, the way we move, the way we dress, the way we feel and the way we express reflect our persona making us each unique and singular.

Original Design Studios

Photoshoot

Original Design Studio is a digital fashion-branding agency specializing in graphic design, photography and social media.

In redesigning the website, we maintained the main aesthetic of the studio: All forms of design – fine art, graphic design and photography intersect with one another and can be combined to achieve original concepts and unique design executions.

Each design platform is presented with particular poses, garments, hair and makeup. The white attire for the model in the white background defines purity of each platform. Her geometric dress, sturdy body language and sharp makeup lines characterize graphic design whereas long sleek dress with soft gestures and organic hair and makeup personify photography. The orange smoke signifying fine art translate the mixture of each platform as it moves in the space combining foreground and background.

Art Direction: Viviksha Panchal and Gina Capozza

Photography: Robert Scheuerman

Model: Anna Ilina
